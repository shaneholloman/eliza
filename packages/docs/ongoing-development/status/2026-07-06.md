## Device-verified findings (captured + reviewed by eye + analyzer)

Web (19 states), iOS simulators, and a physical Moto G Play were captured, OCR'd,
palette-analyzed, and hand-reviewed. `blue=0.000` on every web/iOS state.

- **#14826 (fixed):** native iOS "Open in Eliza?" `UIAlertController` rendered
  system-blue buttons — invisible to the web audit (native system chrome). Set the
  window/appearance tint to brand orange in `AppDelegate`.
- **#14390 (root-caused):** the 4GB Moto "Backend Timeout" is a 5s startup retry
  loop (`getOrCreateDeviceBridgeId`), cloud-only tier with no reachable backend —
  the *designed* error, not a local-agent stall. UX suggestion: distinguish "no
  backend configured → sign in" from "backend hung → retry".
- **Flagged on the #14415 thread:** `--destructive: var(--brand-orange)` — destructive/error
  states share the brand accent hue; owner call whether that is intentional
  mono-orange minimalism or drift. The no-blue rule is otherwise well-enforced
  (`--info`/`--focus`/`--brand-blue` neutralized); the only in-app blue is the
  third-party chain-brand colors in the creator-monetization surface.
